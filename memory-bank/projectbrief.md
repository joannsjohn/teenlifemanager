# Project Brief: Teen Life Manager

## Project Overview
Teen Life Manager is a comprehensive React Native mobile application designed to help teenagers manage various aspects of their lives through a single, integrated platform. The app provides tools for schedule management, volunteering tracking, social life coordination, and mental health support.

## Core Objectives
1. **Empower teenagers** with organizational tools tailored to their unique needs
2. **Promote well-being** through mental health tracking and resources
3. **Encourage community engagement** via volunteering features
4. **Foster social connections** through safe, positive social features
5. **Improve time management** with intuitive scheduling tools

## Target Audience
- Primary: High school students aged 14-18
- Secondary: Parents/guardians (future feature)
- Tertiary: Schools and youth organizations (future integration)

### User Types
1. **Planner**: Needs a timetable to plan and organize their schedule
2. **Volunteer**: Logs hours for school and clubs, tracks progress
3. **Support Seeker**: Prioritizes reflection, motivation, and peer connection

## Platform Requirements
- Cross-platform mobile app (iOS and Android)
- Built with React Native + Expo for rapid development
- Offline-first capabilities (future enhancement)
- Web version support (secondary priority)

## Core Features

### 1. Schedule Management
- **Smart Calendar**: Daily/weekly/monthly views with color-coded time blocks
- **Calendar Sync**: Integration with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and school timetables
- **Task Categorization**: Homework, exams, personal, volunteer activities
- **Smart Reminders**: Custom reminders for assignments or relaxation breaks
- **Color-Coded Time Blocks**: Visual layout for homework, hobbies, self-care, and volunteering

### 2. Volunteering Tracker
- **Volunteer Logbook**: Add hours, descriptions, and location
- **Supervisor Verification**: Generate QR codes or email validation for hours
- **Export Reports**: Download volunteer logs as PDF or Excel for school reporting
- **Leaderboard**: See progress among friends in hours logged (opt-in)
- **Achievement Badges**: Badges for maintaining streaks and completing activities
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress indicators and milestone celebrations

### 3. Peer Support and Community
- **Anonymous Safe Forum**: Teens can talk about stress, volunteering, or school struggles
- **Moderation System**: AI + human moderators prevent bullying or harmful content
- **Private Groups**: Invite-only groups for classmates or clubs
- **Friend Connections**: Connect with peers and share activities
- **Weekly Reflections**: Optional self-review of progress with peers
- **Activity Recommendations**: AI-powered suggestions for social activities

### 4. Mental Health Support
- **Mood Tracker**: Simple daily check-ins with AI-generated mood patterns and insights
- **Smart Journaling**: Text or voice journal with emotional analysis
- **Mindfulness Tools**: Guided breathing exercises and short meditation prompts
- **Wellness Challenges**: Small daily goals like gratitude journaling or physical activity
- **Emergency Support Button**: One-tap access to crisis resources or local hotlines
- **Resource Library**: Articles, videos, and helplines for mental health support
- **Personalized Insights**: AI suggestions for balancing study, rest, and volunteering

### 5. User Accounts and Profiles
- **Authentication**: Sign up/in via email, Google, or Apple ID
- **Profile Setup**: Optional mental wellness and volunteering goals
- **Privacy-First**: Mental health data stored locally or with explicit consent
- **Pseudonymous Profiles**: Allow nicknames displayed to ensure safety and reduce social stress
- **Custom Avatars/Themes**: Reward consistent positive engagement
- **Privacy Controls**: Granular control over what's shared and with whom
- **Theme Customization**: Light/dark mode and custom themes
- **Notification Preferences**: Customizable notification settings

## Success Criteria
1. Intuitive UI/UX that resonates with teenage users
2. Fast, responsive performance on mid-range devices
3. Data privacy and security compliance (COPPA, GDPR)
4. High user engagement and retention rates
5. Positive impact on users' organizational skills and well-being

## Technical Constraints
- Must work on devices running iOS 13+ and Android 8+
- Minimize battery consumption
- Efficient offline data synchronization
- Secure authentication and data storage
- Accessible design (WCAG 2.1 compliance)

## AI and Insights Features
- **AI Suggestions**: Balancing study, rest, and volunteering recommendations
- **Personalized Insights**: Sleep, stress, and emotional balance trend analysis
- **Smart Notifications**: Recommend coping techniques during high-stress patterns
- **Mood Pattern Analysis**: AI-generated insights from mood tracking data
- **Emotional Analysis**: Process journal entries to identify patterns and triggers

## Gamification and Engagement
- **Achievements**: Badges for maintaining streaks, completing exercises, or logging hours
- **Weekly Reflections**: Optional self-review of progress with peers
- **Custom Avatars/Themes**: Reward consistent positive engagement
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual indicators and milestone celebrations
- **Leaderboards**: Friendly competition (opt-in, privacy-respecting)

## Safety and Risk Management

### Known Risks
1. **Over-sharing by teens**: Mitigated through privacy controls and education
2. **Sensitive Content**: Reported posts with moderation system
3. **App tiredness**: Addressed through gamification and varied engagement

### Safety Features
- **Content Moderation**: AI + human moderators for forums and social features
- **Report System**: Easy reporting of inappropriate content or behavior
- **Privacy Controls**: Granular settings for data sharing
- **Age Verification**: Ensure appropriate age group
- **Pseudonymous Profiles**: Reduce social stress with nickname option
- **Emergency Resources**: One-tap access to crisis support

## Future Considerations

- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Voice commands
- Community challenges and competitions
